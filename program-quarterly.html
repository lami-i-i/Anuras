Quarterly Touchpoints

Regular challenges, opportunities, and recognition for girls in our community

What Are Quarterly Touchpoints?

Quarterly Touchpoints are regular, intentional moments of connection designed to keep our community engaged, inspired, and moving forward. Every quarter, we share new challenges, insights, and opportunities curated specifically for girls interested in STEAM.

These touchpoints are designed for girls who want to stay connected to Ànúrà, even if they're not currently enrolled in a formal program. They provide consistent inspiration and actionable next steps.

What You'll Receive

  • Creative Challenges - Monthly bite-sized creative and technical challenges you can complete at your own pace to explore new skills
  • Opportunity Bulletins - Curated scholarship, internship, and grant opportunities from organizations across Africa supporting girls in STEAM
  • Resource Recommendations - Hand-picked learning resources, articles, and tools aligned with current trends in STEAM
  • Community Recognition - Celebrate your progress through our community spotlight feature—share your wins, and we highlight them

How It Works

Every quarter (January, April, July, October):

We send you a curated package via WhatsApp and email that includes:

  • 1 featured challenge for the season
  • 3-5 new opportunities specific to your interests
  • 2-3 curated resources to explore
  • Spotlight stories from girls in our community

You choose how much or how little you engage—this is your space to explore at your own pace.

Why Join?

  • Stay Inspired - Regular reminders that STEAM exploration is for you, with real opportunities in your region
  • Build Your Skills - Challenges help you develop practical skills while exploring areas that interest you
  • Find Opportunities - We do the research so you don't have to—we deliver scholarships and internships directly to you
  • Be Seen - Your progress matters. We celebrate you and help you see your impact

Get Started

Sign up to receive Quarterly Touchpoints by joining our WhatsApp community. We'll add you to our exclusive updates list, and you'll receive your first challenge and opportunity bulletin within 7 days.
