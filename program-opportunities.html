Opportunities Bulletin

Scholarships, internships, grants, and competitions—curated for girls in STEAM

Your Path Forward Starts Here

One of the biggest challenges girls face in STEAM isn't learning the skills—it's finding the opportunities to use them. There are scholarships, internships, competitions, and grants out there that can change your life. But finding them? That's often the hard part.

The Ànúrà Opportunities Bulletin does the research for you. We continuously scan for opportunities across Africa and send them directly to you—saving you time and making sure you never miss out on something that could transform your STEAM journey.

How We Curate Opportunities

We don't just throw every opportunity at you. We specifically look for:

  • Accessibility - Opportunities focused on African girls or girls from underrepresented backgrounds
  • No Hidden Costs - We flag which are free, which have fees, and what's covered
  • Age-Appropriate - We know what age groups are eligible and make that clear
  • Realistic Requirements - We share what's actually required (not just what they say) based on conversations with girls who've applied
  • Diverse Fields - We cover all STEAM fields, from robotics to digital design to environmental science

What Each Bulletin Contains

Every bulletin includes:

  • Deadline This Month: 3-5 opportunities with imminent deadlines you should apply for NOW
  • Upcoming Opportunities: 2-3 opportunities with deadlines 1-3 months away (so you can plan ahead)
  • Application Tips: Advice from girls who've successfully applied, plus common mistakes to avoid
  • Success Story: A profile of one girl from our community who secured an opportunity and what it meant for her
  • Resource of the Month: A guide about the application process, essay writing, or interview prep

Real Examples of Opportunities We've Shared

  • Google Africa Developer Academy - Scholarships + mentorship for young developers across Africa
  • Facebook Women in Engineering - Internship and fellowship programs for underrepresented women
  • African Union Tech Fellowship - Opportunities to work on tech for social impact
  • Design for Africa Grants - Funding for creative projects that solve local problems
  • National Science Olympiad - Competitions with scholarships and recognition

How Often Do You Send These?

Quarterly Bulletins: Four times a year (next to Quarterly Touchpoints), we send comprehensive opportunity bulletins.

Flash Alerts: If we find an especially exciting opportunity with a tight deadline, we send it immediately via WhatsApp.

Timeline: You receive opportunities 1-3 months before deadlines, so you have time to prepare strong applications.

How to Get Started

To receive the Opportunities Bulletin, join our WhatsApp community. We'll automatically include you in our opportunity alert list, and you'll get your first bulletin within 7 days of joining.
