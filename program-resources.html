Your Self-Paced Learning Hub

The Ànúrà Resource Library is a curated collection of beginner-friendly guides, tutorials, and downloadable materials designed to support girls at every stage of their STEAM journey.

Whether you're just starting out or looking to deepen your skills, our resources are organized into five STEAM tracks to make it easy to find exactly what you need.

What's in the Library?

  • Downloadable Guides - Step-by-step PDFs and workbooks you can download and work through at your own pace
  • Video Tutorials - Short, engaging videos from practitioners showing real-world applications
  • Interactive Exercises - Challenges and projects to practice what you've learned
  • Career Exploration Guides - Understand the career paths available in each field and what skills you need
  • External Resources - Curated links to partner platforms like Khan Academy, Codecademy, and Canva

How to Use the Library

Step 1: Choose a STEAM track that interests you—or try them all!

Step 2: Start with the beginner sections. Each resource is labeled with difficulty levels.

Step 3: Download, learn, and create. There's no rush—move at your own pace.

Step 4: Share your projects with our community! We'd love to see what you've created.

Access the Library

All resources in the Ànúrà Resource Library are free and available to members of our community. You can access them anytime, from any device.
