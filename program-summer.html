The SpacePoint Partnership

This July and August, Ànúrà is bringing aerospace technology directly to Lagos. In an exciting partnership with SpacePoint, we are introducing a unique curriculum based on real satellite technology.

Designed for girls aged 11–18, this challenge will utilize specialized challenge cards to teach hands-on, satellite-related tasks, proving that the sky is not the limit—it's just the beginning.

What You'll Experience

  • Guided Tech Workshops Expert facilitators walk you through advanced STEM concepts and creative techniques step-by-step.
  • Satellite & Aerospace Curriculum Engage directly with SpacePoint's challenge cards to learn how real satellite technology operates and solves global problems.
  • Project Collaboration Work in teams to design, build, and present solutions, simulating what real innovation labs and tech companies do.
  • Mentorship Opportunities Connect with women working in STEM fields who will share their experiences, provide feedback on your projects, and offer career advice.
