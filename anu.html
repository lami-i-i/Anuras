What is Ànúrà?

Ànúrà is a social impact initiative that works directly with girls to build confidence and access in science, technology, engineering, arts, and mathematics.

Through hands-on programs, mentorship, and a supportive community, we help girls see themselves as builders, creators, and problem-solvers. We focus on making STEAM learning feel real, relevant, and achievable—not distant or intimidating.

Our Work

Quarterly Touchpoints

Every quarter, Ànúrà releases small activities designed to encourage creativity and exploration in STEAM. These may include challenges, project prompts, or downloadable kits.

Resource Library

Our resource library provides beginner-friendly guides that help girls explore new skills at their own pace. Resources cover design, coding basics, and STEAM careers.

Summer Workshop

The August Summer Workshop is Ànúrà's flagship annual program featuring deeper learning experiences, creative projects, and collaborative activities designed to inspire innovation.

Our Journey So Far

Since 2023, we've worked with 20+ girls through direct programs and built partnerships with organizations committed to education access for underserved communities.

Community

20+ Girls Reached - Through STEM hackathons, virtual programs, and community engagement, we've directly impacted girls across Nigeria and beyond.

Impact

First STEM Hackathon - In August 2024, 18 girls participated in our flagship event in Lagos with keynote speaker Ms. Lola Aworanti-Ekugo (Chief Digital Officer at FBNQuest).

Partners

Strategic Partnerships - Collaborating with Slum2School and The Bloom Africa to ensure access for girls from underserved communities.
