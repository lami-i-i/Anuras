Support Ànúrà

Your support helps Ànúrà continue providing opportunities, resources, and programs that empower girls to explore and thrive in STEAM.

Why Your Support Matters

Donations help expand our ability to create learning experiences, share educational resources, and support future initiatives. Every contribution, no matter the size, makes a meaningful difference in the lives of girls exploring STEAM.

Where Your Donation Goes

We're transparent about how we use donations:

  • Resource Development: Creating educational guides and learning materials
  • Program Support: Organizing workshops, challenges, and community activities
  • Outreach: Reaching more girls and expanding our community
  • Technology: Maintaining platforms and tools that support learning
  • Mentorship: Supporting mentors and facilitators who guide participants
