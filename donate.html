Your support helps Ànúrà continue providing opportunities, resources, and programs that empower girls to explore and thrive in STEAM across Nigeria.

Every contribution directly impacts our ability to create learning experiences, provide safe transportation, and fund intensive workshops. Here is exactly what your support helps us achieve:

₦100k+

Workshop Sponsorship

Directly funds a quarterly touchpoint or summer workshop, providing venue, internet, and mentorship resources.
