Community Building

Connect with girls across Africa who share your passion for STEAM

You're Not Alone

One of the biggest barriers girls face in STEAM isn't the content—it's feeling isolated, like you're the only one interested in tech, science, or innovation. At Ànúrà, we're building a different kind of community: spaces where girls support each other, share ideas, celebrate wins, and navigate challenges together.

Our community spans across Nigeria and Africa, connecting girls aged 10-20 who are exploring STEAM. Whether you're in Lagos, Accra, Nairobi, or anywhere in between, you're part of the Ànúrà family.

What's in Our Community?

  • WhatsApp Community Group - Daily conversations, resource shares, challenge updates, and real-time support from girls just like you
  • Peer Mentorship Matching - Connect with experienced girls or mentors who can guide your STEAM journey based on your interests and goals
  • Challenges & Celebrations - Participate in group challenges and celebrate each other's wins—big and small
  • Cross-Continental Support - Access advice and friendship from girls across Africa with different experiences and perspectives
  • Events & Meetups - Both virtual and in-person gatherings depending on your location and availability

How Our Community Works

WhatsApp Community: This is our daily hub. We share updates on challenges, resources, opportunities, and answer questions in real-time. It's a space to ask for help, celebrate wins, and connect with others.

Peer Mentorship: If you're interested in being matched with a mentor or mentoring others, let us know! We pair girls based on interests, experience levels, and goals.

Events & Activities: Throughout the year, we organize challenges, workshops, and both virtual and in-person meetups. These are chances to deepen friendships and collaborate on real projects.
