Get Involved

There are many ways to support and participate in the Ànúrà mission. Whether you are a student, volunteer, or organization, your involvement helps expand opportunities for girls exploring STEAM.

Join the Community

Become part of the growing Ànúrà community and stay informed about programs, opportunities, and resources. Connect with other girls who share your interests in STEAM.

What you'll get:

  • Quarterly activity prompts and challenges
  • Access to the resource library
  • Community support and encouragement
  • Opportunity alerts and notifications
Get Started

Join Our WhatsApp Community

Stay connected with the Ànúrà family through our WhatsApp group. Share ideas, ask questions, and receive the latest event updates directly in your chat.

In the group you can:

  • Share STEAM experiences and resources
  • Ask questions and get support from peers
  • Learn about upcoming programs and workshops
  • Connect with mentors and volunteers
Join WhatsApp

Volunteer With Us

Support Ànúrà by contributing your skills, ideas, or time to help strengthen our initiatives. Whether you're skilled in design, coding, mentoring, or community building—we'd love your help.

Volunteer roles include:

  • Mentoring and support
  • Content and resource creation
  • Event planning and coordination
  • Community engagement
Volunteer

Partner With Us

Organizations and individuals can collaborate with Ànúrà to expand access to opportunities and resources for girls in STEAM. Partner with us to amplify our impact.

Partnership opportunities:

  • Joint programming and workshops
  • Resource sharing and collaboration
  • Sponsorship and funding
  • Advocacy and awareness
